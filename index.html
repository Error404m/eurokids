About EuroKids MulayamNagar

We Learn Smart Way To Build Bright Futute For Your Children

Pre-school education is crucial in shaping a child’s future by helping them acquire essential skills needed for lifelong learning. EuroKids MulayamNagar aims at nurturing the overall development of a child’s Mind, Body, and Soul, encompassing cognitive, psychomotor, and affective domains. EuroKids offers a comprehensive and mindful learning program focusing on developing your child’s cognitive, psychomotor and affective skills.

Our Programs

We Offer Exclusive Program For Your Kids

EUROTOTS
Toddler Transition Program

Our EuroTots – Toddler Transition Program gently eases toddlers into a structured, play-filled environment, fostering confidence and independence. Through engaging activities, children discover the joy of routine, social interaction, and new experiences while building essential physical, cognitive, and language skills. With every playful step, they grow stronger, more curious, and ready for the world beyond home.

Neha Dwivedi
Principal
Age Group: 1 to 2 years Duration: 12 Months
PlayGroup
About PlayGroup Program

Our PlayGroup program is designed for children aged 2 to 3 years old. Hands-on exploration and discovery are crucial for children in this age group, and we strive to create engaging experiences that promote growth and learning. We understand the importance of this developmental stage in your child's life, and our curriculum is thoughtfully crafted to cater to their needs, providing them with the best possible learning opportunities.

Neha Dwivedi
Principal
Age Group: 2-3 years Duration: 12 Months
Nursery
About Nursery Program

EuroKids Nursery Program emphasizes holistic development, focusing on essential life skills for young learners. Through a carefully crafted curriculum, it enhances learning and fosters interactions among children. It cultivates creativity and nurtures curiosity, providing opportunities for exploration and discovery. The program stimulates imagination through engaging activities such as art & music, encouraging creative expression & talent development.

Neha Dwivedi
Principal
